Many people go their entire lives without finding something that they're truly passionate about, something that truly inspires them. I was fortunate to find that with climbing at a reasonablye early age, when I was 21 back in 2017. Funnily enough, it was actually the MIT PE graduation requirement that led me to discover climbing. There was an option to take a class at a local gym (MetroRock) that fulfilled the requirement, I signed up, and the rest is history.

I have Youtube channel where I upload various outdoor and indoor climbs. It started back in 2020 when I was trying a problem called New Wave (V9) at my local crag, Mortar Rock. There were no videos of anyone doing the problem and it seemed completely impossible. I realized there was a niche to be filled so I resolved to do the problem and capture it on video. If you're a climber as well, I hope my videos can be of use on your journey!
